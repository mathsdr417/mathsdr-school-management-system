# Policies and Compliance

## Overview
This section contains all official policies, regulatory documents, and compliance records that guide school operations, staff conduct, learner welfare, and institutional accountability.

These policies ensure that the school operates in a lawful, ethical, safe, and well-regulated manner in line with national education standards and best practices.

## Scope
The policies in this section cover, but are not limited to:

- School rules and regulations
- Staff code of conduct and employment policies
- Child protection and safeguarding measures
- Health, safety, and welfare policies
- Discipline and behaviour management
- Regulatory and statutory compliance requirements

## Purpose
The purpose of this section is to:

- Provide clear operational guidance for staff and management
- Ensure the safety, welfare, and rights of learners
- Promote ethical conduct and professional standards
- Demonstrate compliance with government and regulatory bodies
- Support inspections, audits, and quality assurance processes

## Governance and Review
All policies are approved by school leadership and are subject to periodic review to ensure relevance, effectiveness, and compliance with updated regulations.

## Usage
Staff members are required to be familiar with and adhere to all applicable policies contained in this section. Copies may be provided to stakeholders where necessary.
