# Health, Safety, and Welfare Policy

## Overview
The school is committed to providing a safe, healthy, and supportive environment for learners, staff, and visitors. Effective health and safety practices are essential to the wellbeing of the school community and the smooth operation of school activities.

## Scope
This policy applies to:
- Learners
- Teaching and non-teaching staff
- Visitors and contractors
- School facilities and activities

## Health and Safety Responsibilities
### School Management
School leadership is responsible for:
- Establishing and maintaining health and safety standards
- Providing safe facilities and equipment
- Ensuring compliance with relevant health and safety regulations
- Investigating accidents and incidents

### Staff Responsibilities
Staff members must:
- Take reasonable care of their own health and safety
- Ensure learner safety during school activities
- Report hazards, accidents, or unsafe conditions promptly
- Follow safety procedures and instructions

### Learner Responsibilities
Learners are expected to:
- Follow school safety rules and instructions
- Use facilities and equipment responsibly
- Report unsafe conditions or incidents to staff

## Accident and Incident Management
- All accidents and injuries must be reported and recorded
- First aid provision must be accessible at all times
- Emergency procedures must be clearly communicated and practiced

## Hygiene and Welfare
The school ensures:
- Clean and hygienic learning environments
- Access to safe drinking water and sanitation facilities
- Measures to prevent the spread of illness
- Support for learner and staff welfare needs

## Emergency Preparedness
- Fire safety measures and evacuation procedures are in place
- Emergency drills are conducted periodically
- Staff and learners are informed of emergency protocols

## Policy Review
This policy is reviewed periodically to ensure ongoing effectiveness and compliance with applicable health and safety regulations.
