# Staff Code of Conduct

## Overview
This Code of Conduct sets out the professional standards, ethical expectations, and behavioural requirements for all staff members of the school.

All employees are expected to uphold these standards in order to maintain a safe, respectful, and effective learning environment.

## Scope
This policy applies to:
- Teaching staff
- Non-teaching staff
- Administrative staff
- Temporary, contract, and support staff

## Professional Behaviour
Staff members must:
- Act with integrity, honesty, and professionalism at all times
- Treat learners, parents, colleagues, and visitors with respect
- Maintain appropriate professional boundaries with learners
- Dress appropriately and present themselves professionally
- Communicate respectfully and responsibly

## Safeguarding and Child Protection
Staff must:
- Safeguard the welfare of all learners
- Avoid any form of abuse, neglect, harassment, or exploitation
- Report safeguarding concerns promptly through approved channels
- Comply fully with child protection policies and procedures

## Attendance and Punctuality
Staff are required to:
- Be punctual and consistent in attendance
- Notify management promptly of any absence
- Fulfil assigned duties responsibly and reliably

## Use of School Property and Resources
Staff must:
- Use school property and resources responsibly
- Protect confidential information and school records
- Avoid misuse of school facilities, funds, or data

## Professional Conduct and Discipline
The following behaviours are prohibited:
- Physical or verbal abuse
- Discrimination or harassment
- Negligence of duty
- Dishonesty or misconduct
- Insubordination or breach of school policies

Breaches of this Code may result in disciplinary action in accordance with school procedures and labour regulations.

## Compliance and Acknowledgement
All staff members are required to read, understand, and comply with this Code of Conduct. Acknowledgement of compliance may be required upon employment and during policy updates.
