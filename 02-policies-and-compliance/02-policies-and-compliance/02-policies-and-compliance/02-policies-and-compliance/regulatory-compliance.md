# Regulatory Compliance Policy

## Overview
The school is committed to full compliance with all applicable education laws, regulations, and guidelines issued by relevant government and regulatory authorities.

Compliance ensures lawful operation, institutional credibility, and protection of the rights of learners and staff.

## Applicable Authorities
The school operates in accordance with requirements set by, but not limited to:
- Ghana Education Service (GES)
- Ministry of Education
- Local government and district education authorities
- Labour and employment regulations
- Health, safety, and child protection regulations

## Compliance Areas
The school ensures compliance in the following areas:
- School registration and approval
- Curriculum delivery and instructional standards
- Teacher qualification and deployment
- Learner welfare and safeguarding
- Assessment and examination practices
- Health, safety, and environmental standards
- Record keeping and reporting obligations

## Roles and Responsibilities
### School Management
- Ensures policies and procedures meet regulatory standards
- Maintains accurate and up-to-date records
- Facilitates inspections, monitoring, and audits

### Staff
- Comply with school policies and regulatory requirements
- Cooperate with inspections and reporting processes
- Maintain professional standards in their duties

## Monitoring and Reporting
- Compliance is monitored through internal reviews and inspections
- Required reports and data are submitted to authorities as stipulated
- Non-compliance issues are addressed promptly and documented

## Policy Review
This policy is reviewed periodically to reflect changes in regulations and to ensure continuous compliance.
