# Child Protection and Safeguarding Policy

## Overview
The school is fully committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare, safety, and rights of all learners. Every child has the right to learn in a safe, secure, and supportive environment free from abuse, neglect, and exploitation.

Safeguarding is the responsibility of all staff members.

## Scope
This policy applies to:
- All learners enrolled in the school
- Teaching and non-teaching staff
- School management and leadership
- Visitors, volunteers, and external service providers

## Principles
The school operates under the following safeguarding principles:
- The best interests of the child are paramount
- All learners are treated with dignity and respect
- Safeguarding concerns are taken seriously and acted upon promptly
- Confidentiality is maintained while ensuring child safety

## Forms of Abuse
Safeguarding concerns may include, but are not limited to:
- Physical abuse
- Emotional or psychological abuse
- Sexual abuse
- Neglect
- Bullying and harassment

## Staff Responsibilities
All staff members must:
- Remain vigilant to signs of abuse or neglect
- Maintain appropriate professional boundaries
- Never engage in behaviour that may endanger a learner
- Report concerns immediately through designated school channels
- Cooperate fully with safeguarding investigations

## Reporting Procedures
- Any safeguarding concern must be reported promptly to school leadership
- Concerns must be documented accurately and confidentially
- Serious cases may be escalated to appropriate external authorities in line with national regulations

## Confidentiality
Safeguarding information is handled with sensitivity and shared strictly on a need-to-know basis to protect learners and staff.

## Training and Awareness
The school ensures that staff receive appropriate safeguarding awareness and training to understand their responsibilities and reporting obligations.

## Policy Review
This policy is reviewed periodically to ensure compliance with current child protection laws, regulations, and best practices.
