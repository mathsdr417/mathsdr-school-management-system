# Policies and Compliance

This section contains all official policies, regulatory documents, and compliance records guiding school operations.

## Scope
- School rules and regulations
- Staff code of conduct
- Child protection and safeguarding policies
- Health, safety, and welfare policies
- Discipline and behaviour management
- Government and regulatory compliance requirements

## Purpose
To ensure lawful, ethical, safe, and consistent school operations in line with national education standards and best practices.
