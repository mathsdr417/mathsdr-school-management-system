# MathsDr School Management System

## Tagline
Practical systems for sustainable school success

## Purpose
This repository provides a structured, mobile-first digital system
for effective school leadership, academic excellence, and operational
continuity in basic schools.

## Core Management Areas
- School governance and policy
- Curriculum and instruction
- Assessment and accountability
- Teacher supervision and development
- Student support and intervention
- Academic planning and reporting
- Administration and operations

## Target Users
- Proprietors
- Headteachers
- Academic supervisors
- School administrators

## Operating Principle
Clarity, structure, accountability, and sustainability.
