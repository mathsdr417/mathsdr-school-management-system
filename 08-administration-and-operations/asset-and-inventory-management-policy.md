# Asset and Inventory Management Policy

## Overview
This policy outlines procedures for the acquisition, use, maintenance, tracking, and disposal of school assets and inventory to ensure accountability and efficient resource utilization.

## Scope
This policy applies to:
- Furniture and equipment
- Teaching and learning materials
- ICT devices
- School vehicles and machinery
- Consumable and non-consumable items

## Asset Acquisition
- Assets are acquired through approved procurement procedures
- All acquisitions must be authorized by school management
- Assets must meet quality and safety standards

## Asset Register
- All assets are recorded in an official asset register
- Asset records include description, serial numbers, location, and condition
- Asset tagging or labeling is required where applicable

## Asset Usage and Custody
- Assets are used strictly for official school purposes
- Responsibility for asset care is assigned to designated staff
- Loss, damage, or misuse must be reported immediately

## Inventory Control
- Periodic inventory checks are conducted
- Stock levels are monitored and replenished as necessary
- Discrepancies are investigated and documented

## Maintenance and Repairs
- Routine maintenance schedules are established
- Faulty assets are reported and repaired promptly
- Maintenance records are maintained

## Disposal of Assets
- Obsolete or damaged assets are disposed of following approval procedures
- Disposal methods comply with safety and environmental standards
- Disposal records are maintained

## Monitoring and Review
- Regular audits of assets and inventory are conducted
- This policy is reviewed periodically to ensure effectiveness
