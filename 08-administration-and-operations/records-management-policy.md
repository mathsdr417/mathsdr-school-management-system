# Records Management Policy

## Overview
This policy defines how school records are created, stored, accessed, protected, and disposed of to ensure confidentiality, accuracy, and regulatory compliance.

## Categories of Records
- Student academic and personal records
- Staff employment and appraisal records
- Financial and accounting records
- Administrative and operational documents
- Regulatory and inspection records

## Record Creation and Maintenance
- Standardized templates and formats
- Accurate and timely data entry
- Responsibility for record updates

## Storage and Security
- Physical and digital storage systems
- Access control and authorization
- Data protection and confidentiality measures
- Backup and disaster recovery procedures

## Retention and Disposal
- Record retention schedules
- Secure disposal methods
- Approval procedures for record destruction

## Monitoring and Review
- Periodic record audits
- Compliance checks
- Policy review and updates
