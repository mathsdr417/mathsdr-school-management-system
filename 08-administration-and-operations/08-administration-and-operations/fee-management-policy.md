# Fee Management Policy

## Overview
This policy outlines the procedures for setting, collecting, recording, and managing school fees to ensure transparency, accountability, and financial sustainability.

## Fee Structure
- Tuition fees
- Admission and registration fees
- Examination fees
- Activity and special programme fees
- Other approved charges

## Payment Procedures
- Approved payment channels (cash, bank, mobile money)
- Issuance of official receipts
- Payment timelines and deadlines
- Penalties for late payment

## Fee Control Measures
- Segregation of duties in fee handling
- Daily and termly reconciliation
- Authorization levels for fee adjustments
- Documentation and record-keeping standards

## Fee Arrears Management
- Identification of outstanding balances
- Parent communication procedures
- Payment plans and agreements
- Sanctions and exemptions (where applicable)

## Reporting
- Daily collection summaries
- Termly financial reports
- Audit trail and supporting documents
