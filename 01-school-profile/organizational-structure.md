# Organizational Structure

## Overview
The organizational structure of the school defines clear lines of authority, responsibility, and communication to ensure effective management and accountability.

## Structure

### Proprietor
Provides overall strategic direction, policy approval, and oversight of school operations.

### Headteacher
Leads daily administration and academic management of the school.

### Academic Leadership
Includes roles such as Academic Supervisor, Curriculum Lead, or Head of Department.

Responsibilities include:
- Supervising teaching and learning
- Monitoring curriculum delivery
- Supporting teacher development

### Teaching Staff
Responsible for classroom instruction, assessment of learners, and implementation of the curriculum.

### Non-Teaching Staff
Support school operations and learner welfare, including:
- Administrative staff
- Accounts and finance
- Security and maintenance
- Support staff

## Reporting Lines
- Teaching and non-teaching staff report to the Headteacher
- Academic leaders support and report to the Headteacher
- The Headteacher reports to the Proprietor

## Communication Flow
Communication flows vertically and horizontally through scheduled meetings, reports, and internal communication channels to ensure coordination and efficiency.
