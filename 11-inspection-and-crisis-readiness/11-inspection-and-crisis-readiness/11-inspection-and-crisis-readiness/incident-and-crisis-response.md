# Incident & Crisis Response Protocol

## Purpose
To provide a clear, calm, and lawful response framework during incidents or emergencies.

---

## Types of Incidents
- Student injury or accident
- Safeguarding concern
- Serious disciplinary incident
- Staff misconduct
- Parent dispute
- External inspection or investigation

---

## Immediate Response
- [ ] Ensure safety of all persons
- [ ] Secure the situation
- [ ] Inform headteacher immediately
- [ ] Record facts only (no assumptions)

---

## Communication
- [ ] Notify proprietor/management
- [ ] Inform parents where necessary
- [ ] Avoid public or informal statements
- [ ] Maintain confidentiality

---

## Documentation
- [ ] Complete incident report
- [ ] Attach witness statements (if any)
- [ ] File all evidence securely

---

## Review & Follow-Up
- [ ] Conduct internal review
- [ ] Implement corrective actions
- [ ] Update policies if required
