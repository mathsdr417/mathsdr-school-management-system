# Parent Complaint Handling Procedure

## Purpose
To resolve complaints fairly, professionally, and transparently while protecting the school.

---

## Complaint Stages
1. Informal discussion
2. Formal written complaint
3. Management review
4. Final decision

---

## Handling Rules
- [ ] Listen without interruption
- [ ] Record complaint accurately
- [ ] Avoid emotional responses
- [ ] Follow documented procedure

---

## Resolution
- [ ] Investigate facts
- [ ] Provide written response
- [ ] Document outcome
- [ ] Monitor recurrence
