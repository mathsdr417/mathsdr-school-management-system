# Inspection Readiness Checklist

## Governance & Leadership
- [ ] School profile and ownership documents available
- [ ] Organisational structure clearly defined
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities documented

## Policies & Compliance
- [ ] All mandatory school policies available
- [ ] Safeguarding policy implemented and evidence available
- [ ] Discipline and grievance procedures documented

## Teaching & Learning
- [ ] Schemes of work available and up to date
- [ ] Lesson plans available
- [ ] Classroom supervision records available

## Assessment & Records
- [ ] Continuous assessment records available
- [ ] Examination scripts and marking schemes filed
- [ ] Result analysis and intervention records available

## Staffing
- [ ] Staff attendance records available
- [ ] Teacher appraisal records available
- [ ] Professional development records available

## Facilities & Safety
- [ ] Health and safety measures documented
- [ ] Incident records available
- [ ] Emergency procedures known by staff
