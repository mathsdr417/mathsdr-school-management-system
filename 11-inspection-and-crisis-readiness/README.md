# Inspection & Crisis Readiness Pack

## Purpose
This module equips the school to respond effectively to inspections, audits, disputes, and emergency situations through pre-prepared documentation and clear response procedures.

---

## Scope
This pack covers:
- Inspection preparedness
- Regulatory compliance verification
- Parent and staff dispute management
- Incident and crisis response
- Evidence-based decision defence

---

## Core Principle
A prepared school does not panic.
Documentation is protection.

---

## Usage
- Keep this folder updated and accessible
- Review termly
- Activate immediately during inspections or incidents
