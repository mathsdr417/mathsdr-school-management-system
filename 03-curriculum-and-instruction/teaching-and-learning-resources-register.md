# Teaching and Learning Resources Register

## Purpose
This register tracks the availability, allocation, and condition of teaching and learning resources used to support instruction.

## Resource Categories
- Textbooks
- Teacher guides
- Charts and visual aids
- Manipulatives and practical materials
- ICT devices and digital resources

## Allocation and Use
- Resources are allocated based on curriculum needs
- Teachers are responsible for proper use and care
- Shared resources must be returned after use

## Monitoring and Maintenance
- Periodic checks of resource condition
- Replacement or repair of damaged materials
- Updates to the register as resources change

## Review
This register is reviewed periodically to ensure instructional needs are adequately supported.
