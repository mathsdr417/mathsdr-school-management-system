# Curriculum and Instruction

This section defines the academic framework, teaching practices, and instructional standards of the school.

## Scope
- Approved curriculum and syllabi
- Scheme of learning and lesson planning
- Teaching methodologies and instructional strategies
- Learning resources and instructional materials
- Classroom management practices
- Monitoring of teaching and learning activities

## Purpose
To ensure structured, effective, and learner-centred instruction that promotes academic excellence and curriculum alignment.
