# Headteacher Weekly Execution Checklist

## Academic Leadership
- [ ] Review lesson notes for all classes (minimum sample: 30%)
- [ ] Conduct at least 2 classroom observations
- [ ] Hold academic briefing with academic lead/supervisor
- [ ] Review learner performance trends and concerns

## Staff Management
- [ ] Verify staff attendance and punctuality records
- [ ] Address at least one staff performance issue or commendation
- [ ] Follow up on previous staff directives

## Student Management
- [ ] Review discipline log and welfare cases
- [ ] Address serious behavioural or safeguarding issues
- [ ] Meet at least one parent or guardian (formal or informal)

## Administration
- [ ] Review weekly timetable compliance
- [ ] Review correspondence and official communication
- [ ] Ensure records are updated and filed

## Compliance & Reporting
- [ ] Identify compliance risks and document actions taken
- [ ] Submit brief weekly report to proprietor/management
