# Examination Readiness Checklist

## Before Exams
- [ ] Timetable approved and published
- [ ] Questions moderated and approved
- [ ] Examination rules communicated
- [ ] Invigilators assigned

## During Exams
- [ ] Attendance recorded
- [ ] Examination conditions enforced
- [ ] Incidents documented

## After Exams
- [ ] Scripts secured
- [ ] Marking completed on schedule
- [ ] Results analysed
- [ ] Interventions planned
