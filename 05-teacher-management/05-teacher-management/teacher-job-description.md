# Teacher Job Description

## Job Title
Teacher

## Reports To
Headteacher / Academic Supervisor

## Job Purpose
To plan, prepare, and deliver effective teaching and learning activities that support learners’ academic, social, and moral development in line with the school’s curriculum and policies.

## Key Responsibilities
- Prepare lesson plans in line with the approved curriculum
- Deliver engaging and learner-centred instruction
- Assess, record, and report learner progress
- Maintain classroom discipline and a safe learning environment
- Identify learners requiring academic or behavioural support
- Participate in staff meetings, training, and school activities
- Maintain accurate academic and administrative records

## Professional Responsibilities
- Comply with the Staff Code of Conduct
- Uphold child protection and safeguarding standards
- Demonstrate punctuality, professionalism, and teamwork
- Communicate effectively with parents and school management

## Qualifications and Experience
- Relevant teaching qualification
- Registration with appropriate teaching authority (where applicable)
- Teaching experience in a basic or secondary school setting (preferred)

## Performance Evaluation
Teacher performance will be evaluated through:
- Lesson observation
- Learner progress outcomes
- Professional conduct and participation
