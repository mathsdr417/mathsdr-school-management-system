# Headteacher Operations Manual

## Purpose
This manual provides clear operational guidance for headteachers to ensure effective school leadership, academic excellence, compliance, and stability.

---

## Scope
This manual covers:
- Daily, weekly, and termly responsibilities
- Academic leadership and supervision
- Staff and student management
- Administration and compliance
- Inspection and crisis readiness

---

## Authority
The headteacher is accountable for the execution of all systems outlined in this manual.

---

## Usage
- Use daily as a reference
- Review weekly and termly
- Apply consistently and professionally
