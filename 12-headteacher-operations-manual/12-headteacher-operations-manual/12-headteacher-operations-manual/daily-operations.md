# Daily Operations Guide

## Academic Oversight
- Confirm lessons are taught according to timetable
- Ensure lesson notes are prepared
- Address instructional issues immediately

## Staff Supervision
- Monitor staff attendance and punctuality
- Address misconduct or absenteeism promptly
- Maintain professional conduct standards

## Student Welfare
- Monitor discipline and safety
- Respond to welfare or safeguarding concerns
- Maintain a safe school environment

## Administration
- Review correspondence
- Ensure records are updated
- Approve urgent decisions

## Professional Conduct
- Model ethical leadership
- Maintain confidentiality
- Communicate clearly and respectfully
