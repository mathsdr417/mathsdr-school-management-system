# Headteacher Authority & Decision Protocol

## Decision Scope
The headteacher has authority over:
- Academic delivery
- Staff supervision
- Student discipline
- Daily operations

## Escalation
Escalate to proprietor when decisions involve:
- Legal issues
- Financial commitments
- Staff termination
- Reputational risk

## Documentation
- Record all major decisions
- Maintain evidence and correspondence
- Act within policy frameworks
