## Governance and Leadership
- School profile documents available and updated
- Clear governance and reporting structure in place
- Policies approved and communicated

## Policies and Compliance
- Staff code of conduct implemented
- Child protection and safeguarding policy enforced
- Health and safety procedures followed
- Discipline policy applied fairly

## Curriculum and Instruction
- Schemes of learning available
- Lesson plans prepared regularly
- Instructional supervision conducted
- Teaching resources adequate and maintained

## Assessment and Examinations
- Continuous assessment records up to date
- Examination records properly maintained
- Result analysis conducted and documented

## Teacher Management
- Appointment letters issued
- Job descriptions available
- Lesson observation and appraisal conducted
- Professional development activities documented

## Student Support and Welfare
- Intervention register maintained
- Literacy and numeracy interventions documented
- Parent engagement records available

## Administration and Operations
- Fee management procedures followed
- Records properly stored and secured
- Asset register maintained and updated

## Reports and Documentation
- Termly reports prepared
- Inspection reports filed
- Previous audit recommendations addressed
